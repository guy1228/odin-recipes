Bavarian Pretzel

A Yummy Bavarian Pretzel from Germany!

This recipe takes takes approximately 80 minutes to complete, and yields 6 large pretzels!

To follow along at home, you will need a stove and an oven

Ingredients

  • 3.5 Cups of Flour
  • 1 Tablespoon of Yeast
  • 1 Teaspoon of Sugar
  • 1.33 Cups of Water
  • 2 Tablespoons of Softened Butter
  • 0.25 Teaspoons of Salt
  • 3 Tablespoons of Baking Soda
  • 2 Tablespoons of Melted Butter
  • Sea Salt to Taste
