Apple & Bacon Grilled Cheese

A Delicious Grilled Cheese with added Apple & Bacon Flavor!

This recipe takes 15 minutes, and yields 1 sandwich

This Recipe was stolen (with love) from AllRecipes.com!

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Slices of Cheddar Cheese
  • 2 Slices of Apple
  • 2 Bacon Strips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your Panini Press.
  2. Set one slice of bread on a work surface. Top bread with 1 slice cheese, apple slices, bacon slices, and remaining 1 slice cheese. Place remaining bread slice on top.
  3. Cook sandwich in the preheated panini press until bread is lightly toasted and cheese begins to melt, about 5 minutes.
